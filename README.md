What is Mockly?
Mockly is an AI-powered mock interview platform designed to help job seekers prepare smarter and perform better in real interviews. It analyzes your resume and the job description you're targeting, then generates a fully personalized interview experience — all in seconds.

Features

Resume-Based Question Generation — Upload your resume and paste a job description. Mockly's AI reads both and generates tailored technical and behavioral questions specific to your experience and the role you're applying for.
AI Mock Interviews — Answer all generated questions in a realistic interview environment. You can switch between technical and behavioral sections, track your progress with a live answer counter, and submit when ready.
Instant AI Evaluation — Once you submit, your answers are evaluated by AI. You get a match score out of 100, detailed feedback on every single answer covering technical accuracy and communication quality, and specific tips on how to improve each response.
Skill Gap Analysis — Mockly identifies exactly which skills you're missing or weak in based on your answers compared to what the job requires.
7-Day Preparation Plan — Based on your performance, Mockly generates a personalized day-by-day preparation roadmap with focus areas, resources, and tasks to get you interview-ready.
Interview Dashboard — Before taking the mock interview, study all generated questions with suggested answers and interviewer intent revealed on demand.
Performance Score — Get an overall match score showing how ready you are for the role, with a visual score ring and performance label.


How It Works

Upload & Describe — Paste the job description, upload your resume PDF, and optionally add a short self-description about your background.
Generate Report — Mockly's AI instantly analyzes everything and generates a full interview report with technical questions, behavioral questions, skill gaps, and a prep plan.
Study — Visit the Interview Dashboard to review all questions, read suggested answers, and understand what the interviewer is really looking for.
Take the Mock Interview — Answer all questions under realistic conditions. Your progress is tracked in real time.
Get Results — Submit your answers and receive an instant AI evaluation with your match score, per-question feedback, skill gaps, and a personalized prep plan to improve before the real interview.
